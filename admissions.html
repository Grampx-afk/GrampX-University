Entry Requirements

Admission Requirements

BSc

Bachelor of Science

  • ✅ Minimum of 5 O'Level credits (WAEC, NECO, or NABTEB) in relevant subjects
  • ✅ Credits must include English Language and Mathematics
  • ✅ Results obtained in not more than two sittings
  • ✅ Sit and pass JAMB-UTME with a minimum score of 170+
  • ✅ Pass the Grampx Post-UTME screening exercise
  • ✅ Duration: 4 years
MSc

Master of Science

  • ✅ A good first degree (minimum Second Class Lower) in a relevant field
  • ✅ Certified transcripts from previous institution
  • ✅ Two academic reference letters
  • ✅ Personal statement of research interest
  • ✅ Relevant work/research experience is an advantage
  • ✅ Duration: 18 months – 2 years
PhD

Doctor of Philosophy

  • ✅ A Masters degree in a relevant field
  • ✅ Research proposal of 1,000–2,000 words
  • ✅ Academic transcripts (all previous degrees)
  • ✅ Two academic referees who are PhD holders
  • ✅ Publication or research experience strongly preferred
  • ✅ Duration: 3 – 5 years

How to Apply

Admission Process

01

Check Requirements

Review the admission requirements for your preferred program (BSc, MSc, or PhD) and ensure you meet all criteria before applying.

02

Download & Complete Application Form

Download the official Grampx University application form or complete the online form below. Fill in all sections accurately.

03

Submit Supporting Documents

Attach all required documents: O'Level results, JAMB result, transcripts, references, and any other materials specified for your program.

04

Screening & Interview

Eligible candidates will be invited for the Post-UTME screening (undergraduate) or an academic interview (postgraduate) at the university.

05

Admission Offer

Successful candidates will receive an official offer of admission via email. Accept your offer and complete the enrollment process before the deadline.
