Our Story

A History of Purpose

Grampx University of Science was founded on 24th January 2008 by a coalition of distinguished scientists, educators, and civic leaders who shared a singular vision: to build an institution that would produce world-class scientists and technologists capable of solving the complex challenges of the 21st century.

What began as a modest campus with three faculties and fewer than 200 students has evolved into a thriving academic community with over 12 faculties, state-of-the-art research laboratories, and a growing body of alumni making meaningful contributions across industries worldwide.

Located at Clock Tower, Bermuda, the university benefits from a unique geographical and intellectual environment that encourages interdisciplinary thinking and international collaboration.

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯

Our Mission

To provide an intellectually stimulating environment that empowers students to excel in science, technology, and innovation through rigorous academic training, ethical leadership development, and a culture of continuous research and discovery.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the leading science and technology university in Africa — a beacon of academic excellence, research innovation, and societal transformation that produces graduates who are globally competitive and locally impactful.
