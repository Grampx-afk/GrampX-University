Beyond the Classroom

Special Academic Programs

🌐

Student Exchange Program

Spend a semester at one of our 14 partner universities across Europe, Asia, and North America. Open to all undergraduate students with a minimum CGPA of 3.5.

🏆

Honours & Excellence Track

High-performing students (CGPA 4.5+) are invited to join the Honours Track — an accelerated academic pathway with mentorship from senior faculty and early research access.

🤝

Industry Internship Program

All undergraduate students complete a mandatory 6-month industrial attachment with one of our 80+ corporate partners before graduation.

📡

Online & Part-Time Learning

Selected postgraduate programs are available in flexible online and part-time formats, designed for working professionals seeking to advance their qualifications.
