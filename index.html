Who We Are

A Legacy of Scientific Excellence

Founded on 24th January 2008, Grampx University of Science has grown from a bold vision into one of Bermuda's most respected institutions of higher learning. We are committed to producing graduates who are not only academically sound but globally competitive.

Our world-class faculty, modern research laboratories, and vibrant student community make Grampx University the ideal environment for intellectual growth and personal development.
